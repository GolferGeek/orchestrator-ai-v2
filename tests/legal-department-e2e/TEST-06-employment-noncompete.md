# EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT
## With Non-Competition and Non-Solicitation Provisions

**Effective Date**: January 20, 2026

This Employment Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into by and between:

**Employer**: TechGiant Corporation ("Company")
**Employee**: _________________ ("Employee")

## 1. POSITION AND DUTIES

**1.1 Position**: Employee is hired as a Software Engineer.

**1.2 Duties**: Employee shall perform such duties as assigned by the Company from time to time.

**1.3 Full-Time Commitment**: Employee shall devote full working time and attention to the Company's business.

## 2. COMPENSATION

**2.1 Base Salary**: $120,000 per year, payable bi-weekly.

**2.2 Benefits**: Employee shall be eligible for standard Company benefits.

**2.3 Bonus**: Employee may be eligible for an annual discretionary bonus.

## 3. EMPLOYMENT AT WILL

Employee's employment is "at will" and may be terminated by either party at any time, with or without cause or notice.

## 4. NON-COMPETITION COVENANT

**4.1 Non-Compete Period**: For a period of TWO (2) YEARS following termination of employment for any reason, Employee shall not:

(a) Engage in, be employed by, consult for, or have any ownership interest in any business that competes with the Company;

(b) Develop, design, produce, market, sell, or provide any products or services that are similar to or competitive with the Company's products or services;

(c) Work for any entity in the technology industry that could potentially compete with the Company in the future.

**4.2 Geographic Scope**: The restrictions in Section 4.1 shall apply WORLDWIDE, without geographic limitation.

**4.3 Definition of Competitor**: A "competitor" includes any company in the following industries:
- Software development
- Cloud computing
- Artificial intelligence
- Data analytics
- Mobile applications
- E-commerce
- Social media
- Internet services
- Hardware technology
- Any other technology-related field

## 5. NON-SOLICITATION

For a period of TWO (2) YEARS following termination, Employee shall not:

**5.1 Employee Non-Solicitation**: Directly or indirectly solicit, recruit, or hire any employee, contractor, or consultant of the Company, or encourage any such person to terminate their relationship with the Company.

**5.2 Customer Non-Solicitation**: Directly or indirectly solicit, contact, or do business with any customer, client, vendor, or business partner of the Company with whom Employee had any contact during employment.

**5.3 Investor Non-Solicitation**: Directly or indirectly contact or solicit any investor, lender, or financial partner of the Company.

## 6. CONFIDENTIALITY

Employee shall maintain the confidentiality of all Company proprietary information indefinitely, both during and after employment.

## 7. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY ASSIGNMENT

**7.1 Work Product**: All inventions, developments, improvements, and works created by Employee during employment, whether or not during working hours, shall be the exclusive property of the Company.

**7.2 Prior Inventions**: Employee represents that Employee has no prior inventions to disclose.

**7.3 Moral Rights Waiver**: Employee waives all moral rights in any work product.

## 8. REMEDIES

**8.1 Injunctive Relief**: Employee acknowledges that breach of Sections 4, 5, or 6 would cause irreparable harm to the Company. The Company shall be entitled to injunctive relief without posting bond.

**8.2 Extension of Restrictions**: If Employee breaches any restrictive covenant, the restricted period shall be extended by the duration of the breach.

**8.3 Forfeiture**: If Employee breaches any provision of this Agreement, Employee shall forfeit any unpaid compensation, bonuses, equity awards, and benefits.

## 9. GOVERNING LAW

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Delaware.

## 10. SEVERABILITY

If any provision is found unenforceable, the court shall modify such provision to make it enforceable to the maximum extent permitted by law.

## 11. ENTIRE AGREEMENT

This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties regarding Employee's employment.
