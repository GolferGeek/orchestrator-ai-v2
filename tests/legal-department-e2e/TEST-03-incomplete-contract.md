# BUSINESS AGREEMENT

**Date**: January 20, 2026

This Agreement is between Company A and Company B.

## 1. PURPOSE

Company A agrees to provide services to Company B. Company B agrees to pay for such services.

## 2. SERVICES

Company A will provide consulting services as requested by Company B from time to time. The specific scope of services will be determined on a project-by-project basis.

## 3. COMPENSATION

Company B will pay Company A for services rendered. Payment terms will be agreed upon for each project.

## 4. CONFIDENTIALITY

Both parties agree to keep confidential any proprietary information shared during the engagement.

## 5. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

Any work product created during the engagement will be owned as agreed by the parties.

## 6. REPRESENTATIONS

Each party represents that it has the authority to enter into this Agreement.

## 7. MISCELLANEOUS

This Agreement may be amended by mutual written consent.
