### Key Financial Use Cases (5-6 minutes)

#### Use Case 1: Revenue Performance Analysis (2 minutes)
**[Recording needed - Content framework ready]**
- **Scenario**: "Analyze our Q4 revenue performance vs Q3 and identify trends"
- **Sample Query**: "Show me quarterly revenue trends, break down by product line, and highlight any concerning patterns"
- **Demo Flow**:
  - **Revenue Analysis**: $2.1M Q4 vs $1.8M Q3 (+16.7% growth)
  - **Gross Profit Calculation**: Revenue minus COGS analysis
  - **Net Margin % Tracking**: Q4: 12.5% vs Q3: 14.2% (margin compression alert)
  - **Product Line Breakdown**: SaaS products +25%, consulting -5%
  - **Trend Identification**: Seasonal uptick in Q4, but margin pressure from increased COGS
- **Agent Response Style**:
  - Uses canonical metrics (Revenue, not Sales)
  - Provides percentage changes and absolute values
  - Flags concerning trends with objective analysis
  - Suggests further investigation areas

#### Use Case 2: Customer Metrics Deep Dive (2 minutes)
**[Recording needed - Content framework ready]**  
- **Scenario**: "Evaluate our customer acquisition efficiency and lifetime value"
- **Sample Query**: "Calculate our current CAC and LTV metrics, analyze the LTV/CAC ratio, and assess retention trends"
- **Demo Flow**:
  - **CAC Analysis**: $450 average customer acquisition cost (up from $380 last quarter)
  - **LTV Calculation**: $2,850 customer lifetime value (18-month average retention)
  - **LTV/CAC Ratio**: 6.33 (healthy ratio above 3.0 threshold)
  - **Churn Rate**: 5.2% monthly churn (industry benchmark comparison)
  - **Retention Cohort Analysis**: 85% 12-month retention rate
- **Key Insights Provided**:
  - CAC increase requires investigation (higher ad costs or lower conversion?)
  - Strong LTV/CAC ratio indicates profitable acquisition
  - Retention rate above industry average suggests product-market fit
  - Recommendation to monitor CAC trend and optimize conversion funnel
