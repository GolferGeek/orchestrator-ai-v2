# Strategic Planning

**Icon:** rocket  
**Color:** #3b82f6  
**Estimated Days:** 14

## Define Team Vision

Establish clear vision for AI adoption.

### ⭐ Conduct team vision session

Gather your team to discuss AI goals. What problems are we solving? What opportunities exist?

### Document strategic objectives

Write clear, measurable objectives. Align with business goals.

### Identify quick wins

Find 2-3 areas where AI can provide immediate value. Focus on high-impact, low-effort opportunities.

### Create roadmap

Plan your AI adoption journey. Set milestones and timelines.

### Define success metrics

Establish KPIs. How will you measure ROI? What does success look like?

## Team Alignment

Ensure everyone understands the direction.

### ⭐ Communicate vision to team

Share your AI strategy with the team. Explain the "why" behind decisions.

### Assign roles and responsibilities

Clarify who does what. Define ownership for different aspects.

### Set up regular check-ins

Schedule weekly or bi-weekly meetings to track progress and address questions.

### Create feedback channels

Establish ways for team members to share ideas, concerns, and successes.
