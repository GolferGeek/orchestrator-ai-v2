# Strategic AI Initiative

**Icon:** rocket  
**Color:** #3b82f6  
**Estimated Days:** 21

## Strategic Foundation

Establish comprehensive AI strategy.

### ⭐ Executive alignment session

Meet with leadership to align on AI vision and priorities. Ensure buy-in and support.

### Define strategic objectives

Document clear, measurable strategic objectives. Align with business goals and KPIs.

### Assess current capabilities

Evaluate your team's current AI capabilities. Identify strengths and gaps.

### Create comprehensive roadmap

Develop a detailed roadmap with phases, milestones, and success criteria.

### Establish governance

Define decision-making processes, roles, and responsibilities for AI initiatives.

## Team Enablement

Enable your team to succeed with AI.

### ⭐ Communicate strategic vision

Share the AI strategy across the organization. Explain the "why" and expected outcomes.

### Allocate resources

Assign budget, people, and infrastructure to AI initiatives. Ensure adequate support.

### Set up success metrics

Define KPIs and measurement frameworks. Track progress regularly.

### Create feedback loops

Establish channels for continuous feedback. Adapt strategy based on learnings.

## Cross-Functional Collaboration

Foster collaboration across teams.

### ⭐ Identify stakeholders

Map all stakeholders affected by AI initiatives. Understand their needs and concerns.

### Facilitate cross-team meetings

Organize regular meetings between teams. Share progress and coordinate efforts.

### Resolve conflicts

Address conflicts and competing priorities. Find win-win solutions.

### Celebrate successes

Recognize and celebrate wins. Build momentum and engagement.
