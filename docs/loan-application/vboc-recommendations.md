VBOCs (Veterans Business Outreach Centers) provide **free** counseling, training, and support to veteran entrepreneurs. They can help with:

## What to Ask VBOCs About Lenders

When you contact the VBOC, ask these specific questions about lenders:

1. **Lender Recommendations:**
   - "Do you have recommendations for SBA-approved lenders for a $275K SBA Express Loan?"
   - "Which lenders in my area have the best rates for veterans?"
   - "Which lenders offer the best veteran benefits (fee waivers, expedited processing)?"
   - "Do you have experience with specific lenders for veteran-owned businesses?"

2. **Lender Comparison:**
   - "Can you help me compare lender options?"
   - "What should I look for when comparing lenders?"
   - "What questions should I ask lenders about veteran benefits?"

3. **Lender Connections:**
   - "Can you provide introductions to lenders?"
   - "Do you have relationships with specific lenders?"
   - "Can you help me contact lenders?"

4. **Application Support:**
   - "Can you review my application before I submit to lenders?"
   - "What can I do to strengthen my application?"
   - "Are there any red flags in my application?"
