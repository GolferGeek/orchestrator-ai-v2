## Phase 1: Foundation & Research (Week 1)

### Goal: Understand requirements and identify resources

#### Task 1.1: Research SBA-Approved Lenders
**Priority:** High  
**Time:** 2-3 hours  
**Status:** ⏳ Pending

**Actions:**
- [ ] Use SBA Lender Match tool: [lendermatch.sba.gov](https://lendermatch.sba.gov)
- [ ] Research lenders with veteran experience
- [ ] Identify lenders offering VetLoan Advantage or similar programs
- [ ] Compare:
  - Interest rates
  - Fee structures
  - Veteran benefits
  - Processing times
- [ ] Create shortlist of 3-5 preferred lenders
- [ ] Document findings in lender comparison spreadsheet

**Deliverable:** Lender comparison spreadsheet with top 3-5 choices

---

#### Task 1.2: Contact Lenders for Veteran Benefits
**Priority:** High  
**Time:** 1-2 hours  
**Status:** ⏳ Pending

**Actions:**
- [ ] Contact each shortlisted lender
- [ ] Ask specific questions:
  - What veteran benefits do you offer?
  - What fee waivers are available for veterans?
  - Do you offer expedited processing for veterans?
  - What is your typical SBA Express approval timeline?
  - What documents do you require?
  - Do you have a VetLoan Advantage program?
- [ ] Document responses
- [ ] Select primary lender based on:
  - Best veteran benefits
  - Fee waiver amounts
  - Expedited processing
  - Responsiveness and service

**Deliverable:** Lender selection with documented benefits

---

#### Task 1.3: Contact Veterans Business Outreach Center (VBOC)
**Priority:** High  
**Time:** 2-3 hours  
**Status:** ⏳ Pending

**Actions:**
- [ ] Find local VBOC: [SBA VBOC Directory](https://www.sba.gov/local-assistance/resource-partners/veterans-business-outreach-center-vboc-program)
- [ ] Schedule consultation appointment (phone or in-person)
- [ ] Prepare questions:
  - Can you review my loan application package?
  - What documents are most critical?
  - Do you have lender recommendations?
  - Can you help with pre-qualification?
  - What common mistakes should I avoid?
- [ ] Attend consultation
- [ ] Take detailed notes
- [ ] Follow up on any recommendations

**Deliverable:** VBOC consultation notes and recommendations
