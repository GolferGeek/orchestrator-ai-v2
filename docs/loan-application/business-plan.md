## 2. Products & Services

### B2B2B Model Overview

We sell to **consulting companies** who offer our solution to their **SMB clients**. This creates a win-win-win:
- **Consulting companies** get a differentiated offering
- **SMB clients** get enterprise-grade AI without enterprise complexity
- **Orchestrator AI** gets distribution through established consulting relationships

### Core Offerings

#### 1. Custom AI Infrastructure Implementation
- **What:** Deploy Orchestrator AI on client servers/cloud
- **Timeline:** 8-12 weeks for complete setup
- **Investment:** $75K-$115K per implementation (vs. $300K+ for enterprise solutions)
- **Result:** Client owns the infrastructure, no ongoing vendor dependency
- **Revenue:** Implementation fees paid by consulting partner or end client

#### 2. Bespoke Agent Development
- **What:** 2-3 custom AI agents built for specific workflows
- **Process:** Requirements gathering → Custom development → Integration → Testing
- **Examples:** Document analysis, customer communication, quality control, research automation
- **Result:** AI that works exactly how the business works
- **Revenue:** Included in implementation fees or separate consulting fees

#### 3. Ongoing Technical Partnership
- **What:** Monthly retainer for maintenance, optimization, and expansion
- **Investment:** $3K-$5K/month (vs. $10K+ for enterprise support)
- **Includes:** System updates, agent refinement, technical advisory
- **Result:** AI gets better over time with expert guidance
- **Revenue:** Ongoing consulting/partnership fees

### Revenue Model

**Per Consulting Partner:**
- **Initial Implementation Fees:** Varies by engagement size ($50K-$100K+)
- **Ongoing Consulting Fees:** $3K-$5K/month retainer
- **Average Annual Value:** $50K-$75K per consulting partner (includes all fees)

**Revenue Breakdown:**
- Implementation fees: One-time per engagement
- Consulting fees: Monthly recurring revenue
- Custom development: Project-based fees
- Total: $50K-$75K annually per consulting partner

### Enterprise Hardening (Current Focus)

**Purpose:** Make platform attractive to consulting companies who need enterprise-ready solutions to offer their clients.

**Phase 1 Hardening ($50K):**
- Database abstraction layer (critical path blocker)
- Auth provider flexibility (SSO/SAML support)
- Initial compliance documentation preparation

**Phase 2 Hardening (Reserved $150K):**
- Additional hardening based on consulting partner feedback
- Compliance certifications (SOC 2, HIPAA) if market demands
- Advanced features based on market validation
- Scaling infrastructure based on partner growth
