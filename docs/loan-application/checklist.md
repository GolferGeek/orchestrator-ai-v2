## Document Organization Tips

### Organization Structure

1. **Create folders:**
   - Personal Documents
   - Business Documents
   - Business Plan Package
   - SBA Forms
   - Additional Documents

2. **Naming Convention:**
   - Use clear, descriptive names
   - Include dates where relevant
   - Example: "Personal_Tax_Return_2023.pdf"

3. **File Formats:**
   - PDF format preferred for all documents
   - Ensure documents are legible and complete
   - Scan documents at high resolution if needed

### Preparation Timeline

**Week 1:**
- Gather all personal documents (tax returns, bank statements, DD-214)
- Obtain credit report
- Collect business legal documents

**Week 2:**
- Complete business financial documents
- Gather technical documentation
- Prepare management team information

**Week 3:**
- Complete SBA forms (with lender assistance)
- Organize all documents
- Review for completeness

**Week 4:**
- Final review and organization
- Submit to lender
- Respond to lender requests promptly
