## Key Assumptions

### Revenue Assumptions
- **First consulting partner acquisition:** Month 3-4 (after $50K marketing investment)
- **Average partner value:** $50K-$75K annually (includes implementation + consulting fees)
- **Partner acquisition cost:** $50K for first partner, then referral-based (minimal cost)
- **Revenue breakdown:**
  - Initial implementation fees: $30K-$50K (one-time per partner)
  - Ongoing consulting fees: $3K-$5K/month ($36K-$60K annually)
  - Total per partner: $50K-$75K annually

### Expense Assumptions
- **Operating expenses:** $9K-$16K/month ($108K-$192K annually)
  - Principal consultant: $5K-$8K/month
  - Technical associates: $2K-$4K/month
  - Infrastructure/hosting: $500-$1K/month
  - Software/tools: $500-$1K/month
  - Marketing (ongoing): $1K-$2K/month
- **Loan repayment:** $3,300/month ($39.6K annually, rounded to $40K)
- **Total annual expenses:** $148K-$232K (including loan payment)

### Business Model Assumptions
- **B2B2B model:** Selling to consulting companies who offer to their SMB clients
- **Gross margin:** 60-70% (lean operations)
- **Target:** 2-4 partners Year 1, 5-10 partners Year 3-4, 10-15 partners maximum
