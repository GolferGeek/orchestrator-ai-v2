## Our Commitment to Development

At Acme Corporation, we believe in continuous learning and development. We invest in our employees' growth because when you succeed, we all succeed. This guide outlines the training and development opportunities available to you.

---

## Learning Philosophy

### Core Principles

- **70-20-10 Model:** 70% on-the-job learning, 20% learning from others, 10% formal training
- **Employee Ownership:** You own your development; we provide the resources
- **Manager Partnership:** Managers support and enable your growth
- **Business Alignment:** Development connects to business needs
- **Continuous Learning:** Learning never stops

## Annual Development Budget

### Individual Budget

Every full-time employee receives an annual professional development budget:

| Level | Annual Budget |
|-------|---------------|
| IC1-IC2 | $1,500 |
| IC3-IC4 | $2,500 |
| IC5+ | $3,500 |
| Manager (M1-M2) | $3,000 |
| Director+ (M3+) | $4,000 |

### Eligible Expenses

- Conferences and workshops
- Certifications and exams
- Books and learning materials
- Online courses (beyond company-provided)
- Professional memberships
- External training programs

### How to Use

1. Discuss plans with your manager
2. Get manager approval
3. Submit expense through Concur
4. Attach receipts and approval

Budget resets January 1. Unused funds do not carry over.

## Career Development

### Career Paths

Defined career paths exist for major functions:
- Engineering (IC and Management tracks)
- Product Management
- Sales
- Customer Success
- Marketing
- Finance
- HR

Career path documents available on HR portal.

### Career Conversations

Managers and employees should have career conversations:
- During annual review
- During mid-year check-in
- Ongoing as part of regular 1:1s

**Discussion Topics:**
- Short-term goals (1 year)
- Long-term aspirations (3-5 years)
- Skills to develop
- Experiences to gain
- Support needed

### Individual Development Plans (IDPs)

Create an IDP with your manager:
1. Assess current state
2. Define development goals
3. Identify actions and resources
4. Set timeline and milestones
5. Review progress regularly

IDP template available on HR portal.

### Job Rotations

Explore different roles through:
- Temporary assignments (3-6 months)
- Cross-functional projects
- Stretch assignments
- Job shadowing

Discuss opportunities with your manager.

## Tuition Reimbursement

### Eligibility

- Full-time employees
- 1+ year of service
- Manager approval
- Degree or program related to job or career growth

### Benefit

| Degree Level | Annual Maximum |
|--------------|----------------|
| Undergraduate | $5,250 |
| Graduate | $5,250 |
| Professional Certification | $3,000 |

### Requirements

- Pre-approval required
- Accredited institution
- Minimum grade: B (undergraduate), B+ (graduate)
- 2-year service commitment after completion

### Process

1. Complete Tuition Reimbursement Request Form
2. Obtain manager approval
3. Submit to HR before classes begin
4. Complete coursework
5. Submit grades and receipts
6. Receive reimbursement
