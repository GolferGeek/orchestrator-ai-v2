## 1. Overview

Acme Corporation's incentive programs reward employees for individual performance, team achievements, and company success. This document outlines the various bonus and incentive programs available.

---

## 2. Annual Performance Bonus

### 2.1 Eligibility

**Eligible:**
- Full-time employees at IC3 level and above
- Must be employed on December 31 of the performance year
- Must be employed on payment date (typically March)
- Hired before October 1 of performance year

**Not Eligible:**
- Employees below IC3 level
- Part-time employees
- Contractors and temporary workers
- Employees on Performance Improvement Plans
- Employees who resigned or were terminated before payment

### 2.2 Target Bonus by Level

| Level | Target Bonus (% of Base Salary) |
|-------|--------------------------------|
| IC3 | 5% |
| IC4 | 8% |
| IC5 | 12% |
| M1 | 10% |
| M2 | 15% |
| M3 | 20% |
| M4 | 25% |
| E1 (VP) | 35% |
| E2 (SVP/C-Suite) | 50% |

### 2.3 Bonus Calculation

The annual bonus is calculated using three components:

**Formula:**
```
Bonus = Target Bonus × Company Factor × Department Factor × Individual Factor
```

**Component Weights:**
| Component | Weight |
|-----------|--------|
| Company Performance | 50% |
| Department Performance | 25% |
| Individual Performance | 25% |

**Multiplier Ranges:**
| Performance | Multiplier |
|-------------|------------|
| Below Threshold | 0% |
| Threshold | 50% |
| Target | 100% |
| Maximum | 150% |

### 2.4 Performance Year

- Performance year: January 1 - December 31
- Bonus earned based on performance year results
- Payment: March following performance year (e.g., 2025 bonus paid March 2026)

### 2.5 Proration

Bonuses prorated for:
- New hires (based on months employed)
- Internal transfers (weighted by time in each role)
- Employees on leave (based on months actively working)

### 2.6 Company Performance Metrics

Company performance is based on achievement against annual goals:

**2025 Company Goals:**
| Metric | Threshold (50%) | Target (100%) | Max (150%) | Weight |
|--------|----------------|---------------|------------|--------|
| Revenue | $500M | $550M | $600M | 40% |
| Operating Income | $75M | $90M | $105M | 30% |
| Customer NPS | 45 | 55 | 65 | 15% |
| Employee Engagement | 70% | 78% | 85% | 15% |

### 2.7 Department Performance

Each department has specific goals set by leadership. Examples:
- Sales: Quota attainment, new customer acquisition
- Engineering: Product delivery, quality metrics
- Customer Success: Retention rate, expansion revenue
- Operations: Efficiency metrics, cost savings

### 2.8 Individual Performance

Based on annual performance review rating:

| Rating | Multiplier |
|--------|------------|
| Exceptional | 125% - 150% |
| Exceeds Expectations | 100% - 125% |
| Meets Expectations | 75% - 100% |
| Needs Improvement | 0% - 50% |
| Unsatisfactory | 0% |

## 3. Sales Incentive Compensation

### 3.1 Overview

Sales roles have separate commission plans with on-target earnings (OTE).

### 3.2 Account Executive Plan

**Structure:**
- Base salary: 50% of OTE
- Variable compensation: 50% of OTE (uncapped)

**OTE by Level:**
| Level | On-Target Earnings |
|-------|-------------------|
| AE I | $130,000 |
| AE II | $180,000 |
| Senior AE | $240,000 |
| Strategic AE | $320,000 |

**Commission Components:**
| Component | Weight | Payment |
|-----------|--------|---------|
| New Business | 70% | 10% of first-year ACV |
| Expansion | 20% | 8% of expansion ACV |
| Renewals | 10% | 3% of renewal ACV |

**Accelerators:**
| Quota Attainment | Rate |
|------------------|------|
| 0-50% | 50% of normal rate |
| 50-100% | 100% of normal rate |
| 100-125% | 125% of normal rate |
| 125%+ | 150% of normal rate |

### 3.3 Sales Engineer Plan

**Structure:**
- Base salary: 70% of OTE
- Variable compensation: 30% of OTE

**OTE by Level:**
| Level | On-Target Earnings |
|-------|-------------------|
| SE I | $150,000 |
| SE II | $185,000 |
| Senior SE | $220,000 |
| Principal SE | $260,000 |

**Variable Components:**
| Component | Weight |
|-----------|--------|
| Team Quota Attainment | 60% |
| Individual POC Success | 25% |
| Technical Certifications | 15% |

### 3.4 Sales Management

**Structure:**
- Base salary: 60% of OTE
- Variable compensation: 40% of OTE

**Variable Components:**
| Component | Weight |
|-----------|--------|
| Team Quota Attainment | 70% |
| Individual Contribution | 15% |
| Team Development Metrics | 15% |

### 3.5 Commission Payment Schedule

- Monthly draw: Portion paid monthly based on pipeline
- Quarterly true-up: Reconciled against actual results
- Annual adjustment: Final reconciliation in Q1

### 3.6 Clawback Provisions

Commissions may be clawed back for:
- Cancelled deals within 90 days
- Customer churn within first year
- Fraudulent deals
- Material misrepresentation

## 4. Spot Bonuses

### 4.1 Purpose

Recognize exceptional contributions outside normal job responsibilities.

### 4.2 Award Amounts

| Category | Amount Range |
|----------|-------------|
| Standard | $500 - $1,000 |
| Significant | $1,000 - $2,500 |
| Exceptional | $2,500 - $5,000 |

### 4.3 Criteria

Spot bonuses are for:
- Exceptional project contributions
- Going above and beyond
- Key customer wins or saves
- Process improvements with measurable impact
- Crisis response
- Filling in for extended absences

### 4.4 Approval Requirements

| Amount | Approver |
|--------|----------|
| Up to $1,000 | Manager |
| $1,000 - $2,500 | Director + HR |
| $2,500 - $5,000 | VP + HR |

### 4.5 Process

1. Manager completes Spot Bonus Nomination Form
2. Describe specific achievement and impact
3. Submit for approval
4. HR processes approved nominations
5. Payment on next payroll (or separate check)
6. Recognition communication (with employee permission)

### 4.6 Limits

- Maximum 2 spot bonuses per employee per year
- Department budget limits apply
- Not to be used in place of annual bonus

## 5. Referral Bonus Program

### 5.1 Overview

Employees receive bonuses for referring candidates who are hired.

### 5.2 Referral Amounts

| Position Level | Referral Bonus |
|----------------|----------------|
| IC1-IC2 | $2,000 |
| IC3-IC4 | $3,500 |
| IC5+ | $5,000 |
| Manager (M1-M2) | $4,000 |
| Director+ (M3+) | $7,500 |
| Executive (E1+) | $10,000 |

### 5.3 Hot Jobs Bonus

Critical open positions may offer enhanced bonuses:
- 1.5x standard bonus
- Listed on HR portal as "Hot Jobs"
- Time-limited offers

### 5.4 Eligibility

**Eligible Referrers:**
- All full-time employees
- Part-time employees (20+ hours/week)
- Hired employee must stay 90 days

**Not Eligible:**
- Recruiters and HR (for positions in their scope)
- Hiring managers (for their own positions)
- Executives (for their direct reports)
- Temporary workers and contractors

### 5.5 Payment Schedule

| Milestone | Payment |
|-----------|---------|
| Hire date | 50% of bonus |
| 90 days employed | Remaining 50% |

### 5.6 How to Submit

1. Log referral in Workday before candidate applies
2. Candidate notes referral on application
3. If hired, HR processes bonus automatically

## 7. Long-Term Incentive (Equity)

### 7.1 Stock Options

See 401(k) and Compensation Policy for equity details.

**Summary:**
- Initial grants at IC4+ and M2+ levels
- 4-year vesting with 1-year cliff

## 9. Tax Information

### 9.1 Tax Treatment

All bonuses are considered taxable income:
- Subject to federal and state income tax
- Subject to Social Security and Medicare
- Withheld at supplemental income rate (22% federal)

### 9.2 Timing

Bonuses are taxable in the year paid, not earned.

### 9.3 Gross-Up

Some awards may be grossed-up to cover taxes:
- Relocation bonuses
- Certain executive awards
- Documented in offer letter or award agreement

Bonus amounts are confidential. Employees should not share their bonus information except:
- With HR or manager
