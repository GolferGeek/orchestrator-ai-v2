# Law Firm RAG Collection

Sample legal documents for demonstrating RAG agents in law firm settings.

**DISCLAIMER**: These are fictional sample documents for demonstration purposes only. Not legal advice.

## Document Categories

| Folder | Contents | Use Case |
|--------|----------|----------|
| `contracts/` | Contract templates, NDAs, engagement letters | "What are our standard NDA terms?" |
| `client-intake/` | Intake forms, conflict check procedures | "What info do we need for a new PI client?" |
| `firm-policies/` | Billing, ethics, confidentiality policies | "What's our policy on contingency fees?" |
| `litigation/` | Pleading templates, discovery checklists | "What's the timeline for a motion to dismiss?" |
| `estate-planning/` | Will templates, trust documents, checklists | "What documents are needed for a basic estate plan?" |

## Sample Queries

Once loaded into a RAG agent, test with:

- "What are the key terms in our standard NDA?"
- "How do we handle conflicts of interest?"
- "What's included in our estate planning package?"
- "What are our billing rates for litigation matters?"
- "What information do we collect during client intake?"
