## CLIENT RESPONSIBILITIES

To enable us to represent you effectively, you agree to:

1. **Communicate promptly** – Respond to our requests for information and documents in a timely manner
2. **Be truthful** – Provide complete and accurate information about your matter
3. **Keep us informed** – Notify us immediately of any developments related to your matter
4. **Attend meetings** – Appear at scheduled appointments, depositions, hearings, and trial
5. **Pay timely** – Pay invoices within the agreed timeframe
6. **Make decisions** – Provide direction on settlement offers and major decisions (these decisions are ultimately yours to make)

## CONFIDENTIALITY

All communications between us are protected by attorney-client privilege. We will not disclose confidential information without your consent, except as required by law or court order, or as necessary to provide legal services.

## DOCUMENT RETENTION

Upon conclusion of your matter, we will return original documents to you. We retain client files for a period of seven (7) years, after which files may be destroyed without further notice.

## TERMINATION

Either party may terminate this engagement at any time by written notice. If you terminate our services, you remain responsible for fees and costs incurred through the date of termination. If we withdraw from representation, we will take reasonable steps to protect your interests and will provide reasonable time for you to obtain new counsel.

## DISPUTE RESOLUTION

Any fee disputes will first be submitted to the State Bar fee arbitration program. Any other disputes arising from this engagement shall be resolved through binding arbitration in accordance with the rules of the American Arbitration Association.

## NO GUARANTEE OF OUTCOME

We will use our best professional efforts on your behalf, but we cannot and do not guarantee any particular outcome. The legal system involves many uncertainties, and results depend on facts and circumstances beyond our control.
