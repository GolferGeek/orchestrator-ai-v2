# Fee Agreement Policy

**Smith & Associates, PLLC**
**Effective Date: January 1, 2024**
**Last Updated: January 1, 2024**

## Purpose

This policy establishes the firm's standards for fee agreements, billing practices, and client financial arrangements.

## Fee Structures

### Hourly Rates

Standard hourly rates by attorney level:

| Level | Rate |
|-------|------|
| Senior Partner | $450/hour |
| Junior Partner | $350/hour |
| Senior Associate | $275/hour |
| Junior Associate | $200/hour |
| Paralegal | $125/hour |
| Legal Assistant | $85/hour |

### Contingency Fees

Contingency fee arrangements are permitted for:
- Personal injury cases
- Employment discrimination claims
- Some contract disputes (with partner approval)

**Standard Contingency Rates:**
- 33.33% if settled before litigation
- 40% if settled after litigation commenced
- 45% if case goes to trial

### Flat Fee Arrangements

Flat fees are approved for:
- Simple wills: $500-$1,500
- Power of Attorney: $250-$400
- LLC Formation: $750-$1,500
- Uncontested divorce: $1,500-$3,000
- Real estate closing (buyer): $800-$1,200
- Real estate closing (seller): $600-$900

### Retainer Requirements

**Minimum Retainers by Matter Type:**
- General litigation: $5,000
- Complex litigation: $15,000
- Corporate transactions: $7,500
- Family law (contested): $5,000
- Criminal defense (misdemeanor): $2,500
- Criminal defense (felony): $10,000

## Billing Practices

### Time Entry Requirements

- All time must be entered within 24 hours
- Minimum billing increment: 0.1 hours (6 minutes)
- Descriptions must be detailed and client-facing appropriate
- Travel time billed at 50% of standard rate

### Invoice Schedule

- Monthly billing cycle (1st-30th/31st)
- Invoices generated by 5th of following month
- Payment due within 30 days
- Interest of 1.5% per month on balances over 60 days

### Trust Account Requirements

- All retainers deposited to IOLTA trust account
- Funds transferred to operating account only after services rendered
- Monthly trust account reconciliation required
- Client notified when retainer falls below 25% of original amount

## Client Communication

### Fee Disclosure

All fee agreements must include:
1. Scope of representation
2. Fee structure (hourly, contingency, flat)
3. Expense policy
4. Retainer requirements
5. Billing frequency
6. Payment terms
7. Termination provisions

### Written Agreement Required

Written fee agreements are **mandatory** for:
- All new client matters
- Contingency fee arrangements
- Matters exceeding $1,000 in anticipated fees
- Any litigation matter

## Expense Policy

### Reimbursable Expenses

Clients are responsible for:
- Filing fees and court costs
- Deposition costs
- Expert witness fees
- Travel expenses (mileage at IRS rate)
- Postage and courier services
- Photocopies ($0.25/page)
- Long-distance telephone
- Online research (at cost)

### Advance Required

For expenses exceeding $500, advance payment is required before incurring the expense.

## Collections

### Past Due Accounts

- 30 days: Reminder statement
- 60 days: Phone call from billing coordinator
- 90 days: Letter from supervising attorney
- 120 days: Matter placed on hold pending payment
- 180 days: Consider withdrawal and/or collections

### Write-Offs

Write-offs require:
- Under $500: Associate approval
- $500-$2,500: Partner approval
- Over $2,500: Management Committee approval

## Exceptions

Any deviation from this policy requires written approval from a managing partner.
