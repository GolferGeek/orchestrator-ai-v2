## What Is the EAP?

The Employee Assistance Program (EAP) is a free, confidential resource to help you and your family navigate life's challenges. Whether you're dealing with stress, relationship issues, financial concerns, or other personal matters, the EAP is here to help.

**Cost:** FREE to all employees and their household members
**Availability:** 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year
**Confidentiality:** Your use of EAP services is completely confidential

---

## Who Is Eligible?

- All Acme Corporation employees (full-time, part-time, temporary)
- Spouses and domestic partners
- Dependent children
- Anyone living in your household

No enrollment required. Services available immediately upon hire.

## Confidentiality

### Your Privacy Is Protected

- No one at Acme will know you used EAP services
- Your information is not shared with your employer
- Counseling records are separate from your personnel file
- Protected by federal and state confidentiality laws

### Exceptions

Information may be disclosed only when:
- You provide written consent
- Required by law (e.g., child abuse reporting)
- Immediate danger to yourself or others
- Court order

### What Acme Sees

Acme receives only aggregate, anonymous utilization data:
- Overall number of employees using services
- Types of services used (categories only)
- No individual names or details

Consider referring employees when you notice:
- Performance changes
- Attendance issues
- Behavioral changes
- Interpersonal conflicts
- Signs of stress or distress
- Life events affecting work

### How to Make a Referral

**Informal Referral:**
- Mention EAP as a resource
- Provide contact information

- Follow up supportively

- For significant performance issues

- Suggest EAP as a resource

**Mandatory Referral:**
- Required as condition of employment
- Typically part of disciplinary process

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Is EAP really free?**
A: Yes. Acme pays for all EAP services. There's no cost to you.

**Q: Will my manager know I used the EAP?**
A: No. Your use of EAP is confidential. Acme receives only anonymous, aggregate data.

**Q: How many counseling sessions do I get?**
A: Up to 8 sessions per issue, per year. If you need more, EAP can help you find long-term care.

**Q: Can my family use the EAP?**
A: Yes. Your spouse/partner, dependents, and household members can all use EAP services.

**Q: Do I need to enroll?**
A: No enrollment needed. Just call 1-800-555-HELP or visit the website.

**Q: What if I need help after hours?**
A: EAP is available 24/7/365. Call anytime.

**Q: Can I choose my own counselor?**
A: EAP will match you with a counselor in your area. You can request a different counselor if needed.

**Q: Is video counseling available?**
A: Yes. You can choose in-person, video, or phone counseling.
