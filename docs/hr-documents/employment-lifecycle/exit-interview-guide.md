## Purpose

Exit interviews help Acme Corporation understand why employees leave and identify opportunities to improve the workplace. Your honest feedback is valuable and helps us become a better employer.

---

## Overview

**What:** A confidential conversation about your experience at Acme

**When:** During your final week of employment

**Duration:** 30-45 minutes

**Format:** In-person, video call, or written questionnaire

**Conducted By:** HR representative (not your manager)

**Participation:** Voluntary but encouraged

---

## What to Expect

### The Interview

The exit interview is a structured conversation covering:
- Your reasons for leaving
- Your experience at Acme
- Your relationship with your manager
- Your views on the work environment
- Suggestions for improvement

### Confidentiality

- Your responses are confidential to HR
- Feedback is aggregated and anonymized before sharing
- Specific concerns may be addressed, but not attributed to you
- Your manager will not see your individual responses

### Your Rights

- Participation is voluntary
- You may skip questions you prefer not to answer
- You may request a different HR interviewer
- You may provide feedback in writing instead

## Exit Interview Questions

### Reason for Leaving

1. What is your primary reason for leaving Acme Corporation?
   - [ ] Better opportunity/career advancement
   - [ ] Higher compensation
   - [ ] Relocation
   - [ ] Work-life balance
   - [ ] Relationship with manager
   - [ ] Company culture
   - [ ] Job dissatisfaction
   - [ ] Personal reasons
   - [ ] Retirement
   - [ ] Other: _______________

2. What factors most influenced your decision to leave?

3. Was there a specific event or situation that triggered your decision?

4. Could anything have been done to prevent your departure?

### Job Satisfaction

5. Overall, how satisfied were you with your job at Acme?
   - [ ] Very satisfied
   - [ ] Satisfied
   - [ ] Neutral
   - [ ] Dissatisfied
   - [ ] Very dissatisfied

6. What did you enjoy most about your job?

7. What did you enjoy least about your job?

8. Did your job meet your expectations when you were hired?

9. Were your skills and talents fully utilized?

10. Did you have the resources and tools needed to do your job effectively?

### Manager Relationship

11. How would you describe your relationship with your direct manager?

12. Did your manager provide adequate feedback and recognition?

13. Did your manager support your professional development?

14. Did you feel comfortable bringing concerns to your manager?

15. How would you rate your manager's effectiveness?
    - [ ] Excellent
    - [ ] Good
    - [ ] Average
    - [ ] Below average
    - [ ] Poor

### Career Development

16. Were there sufficient opportunities for growth and advancement?

17. Did you receive the training and development you needed?

18. Were your career goals discussed and supported?

19. What could Acme have done to help you grow professionally?

### Compensation and Benefits

20. How competitive did you find your compensation?
    - [ ] Very competitive
    - [ ] Competitive
    - [ ] About average
    - [ ] Below average
    - [ ] Not competitive

21. Were you satisfied with your benefits package?

22. Did compensation play a role in your decision to leave?

### Work Environment

23. How would you describe the work culture at Acme?

24. Did you feel valued and respected?

25. How was the work-life balance?

26. Did you experience or witness any concerning behaviors (harassment, discrimination, etc.)?

27. How effective was communication within your team and department?

28. How effective was communication from leadership?

### New Position (If Applicable)

29. Where are you going? (Optional)

30. What attracted you to your new opportunity?

31. What is your new role offering that Acme did not?

### Final Thoughts

32. What advice would you give to your replacement?

33. What advice would you give to Acme leadership?

34. Would you consider returning to Acme in the future?
    - [ ] Yes
    - [ ] Maybe
    - [ ] No

35. Would you recommend Acme as an employer to others?
    - [ ] Definitely
    - [ ] Probably
    - [ ] Not sure
    - [ ] Probably not
    - [ ] Definitely not

36. On a scale of 0-10, how likely are you to recommend Acme as a place to work? ___

37. Is there anything else you'd like to share?

## Tips for a Productive Exit Interview

### Do:

- **Be honest:** Your candid feedback helps us improve
- **Be specific:** Share concrete examples when possible
- **Be constructive:** Focus on what could be better
- **Be professional:** Maintain a respectful tone
- **Be complete:** Cover all areas of your experience

### Don't:

- **Don't vent:** Focus on constructive feedback, not complaints
- **Don't burn bridges:** You may cross paths with colleagues again
- **Don't exaggerate:** Stick to facts and real experiences
- **Don't settle scores:** This isn't the time for personal grievances
- **Don't feel obligated:** Skip questions you're uncomfortable answering

## What Happens with Your Feedback

### Immediate Actions

If you report:
- Safety concerns → Immediately escalated to appropriate team
- Harassment or discrimination → Investigated per policy
- Legal or ethical violations → Reported to appropriate parties

### Aggregate Analysis

Your feedback is combined with other exit interviews to:
- Identify trends and patterns
- Highlight areas for improvement
- Inform HR and leadership initiatives
- Measure engagement drivers

### Reporting

- Quarterly summaries shared with leadership
- Department-specific insights shared with leaders
- No individual attribution
